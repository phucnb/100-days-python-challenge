JOB TITLE: Application Engineer

REPORTING TO: Business Development Manager

GENERAL DESCRIPTION: The top priorities facing the successful applicant will be to generate connector design proposals and cost matrix for new and existing programs, to possess complete understanding of ACCs product lines and capabilities and provide technical support to field sales and intra-company personnel. The ideal candidate will be a self-confident, detailed-oriented individual that possesses persistence. Performance will be measured by timely response and innovation of design proposals and accuracy of cost estimates. Position requires significant people contact and significant pressure to increase design wins and order/sales growth.


Must Have:

  • Engineering degree.
  • 3 5 years experience
  • Computer literate with MS Office and design software (Solidworks or other 3D modeling)
  • Excellent communication skills.
  • Knowledge of cost and selling price.
  • No restrictions on international travel.
  • Excellent presentation skills.

Desired:

  • Connector design experience.
  • Mechanical engineering or Technologist experience.

JOB DUTIES:

  • Design proposals to customers.
  • Review customer specifications.
  • Generate cost matrix and present to management.
  • Launch new programs or products.
  • Works with other marketing personnel, engineering, purchasing and new programs coordinator.
  • Travel with sales team to increase business in region.
  • Identify and analyze potential new markets/customers for new and/or existing products.

ASPECTS OF POSITION:

  • Customer satisfaction oriented.
  • Team related effort- teamwork.
  • Working with new technology, groundbreaking programs.
  • Autonomy within own functions
  • Heavy workload and short deadlines for proposals.
  • Make quick decisions
